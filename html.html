Competitive Games

Splatoon 3 is currently the game that I play the most because it recently came out a few weeks ago. I have played both Splatoon 1 and 2 in the past. This game is a fast paced shooter game that has an emphasise on teamwork. Splatoon 2 is the first game where I played competitive seriously. I have been to join teams and make multiple friends in the splatoon community during COVID-19. I plan to continue to participate in tournaments and compete till I can reach the top with my team.

Casual Games

Pokemon Masters EX is my favourite game to play on my phone. It is a mobile gacha game where you pull for pairs of a Pokemon and a trainer. I enjoy the battling mechanics of the game along with collecting my favourite trainers as most of them come from games I played when I was young. It is the most convenient game for me to play as I have my phone on me more often than my Nintendo Switch. If I am seen playing a game on my phone, 99% of the time it is Pokemon Masters EX.

Kirby and the Forgotten Land is the most recent mainline game in the Kirby franchise. I love all Kirby games. I started playing Kirby games when I was in Grade 3. Since then, I have played every Kirby game released since then. I love the simplicity, cute characters designs, and fun platforming gameplay. Kirby and the Forgotten Land is a great game for me to play when I want to relax.
